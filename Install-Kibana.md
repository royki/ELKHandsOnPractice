##### _Install Kibana in Debien_

- `curl -L -O https://artifacts.elastic.co/downloads/kibana/kibana-6.2.1-linux-x86_64.tar.gz`
- `tar xzvf kibana-6.2.1-linux-x86_64.tar.gz`

##### _Start Kibana_
-`./bin/kibana`
